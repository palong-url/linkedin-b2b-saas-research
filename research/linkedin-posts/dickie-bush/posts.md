## Post 5

My daily routine 6 years ago:
1. Screeching 5AM alarm
2. Immediately caffeinate
3. Have a pathetic morning workout
4. Work under fluorescent lights for 12 hours
5. Take freezing cold NYC subway home
6. Grab a 6 pack and sit on the couch
7. Smile & say I'm living the dream

But here's my current routine:
1. Wake up without an alarm
2. 20 minute walk to cafe without my phone
3. Journal for 30 minutes to clarify my thoughts
4. Write for 90 minutes on iPad (with no WiFi)
5. Take a 15-minute walking break through my Miami neighborhood
6. Write for another 90 minutes
7. Eat 8 oz of steak + vegetables, sweep Slack with team
8. Drive forward critical projects, take meetings
9. Walk to gym + lift weights
10. Walk during sunset with dog and girlfriend, cook ribeye on the terrace

Everything I ever wanted in this world happening every single day. You can either choose to change or choose to remain the same. And if you want to change, today is the perfect day to begin.
