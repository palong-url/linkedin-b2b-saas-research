## Post 5

Every client I coach gets 3 "musts" from me. To share, showcase, and sell.

1. Share your hard-earned knowledge with others. 1 post = 1 topic = 1 takeaway. (Personally, I always say "share it all")

2. Showcase your work. A lot of beginners on LinkedIn would swap their career with yours in a heartbeat. So why aren't you talking about your results? You MUST. (My entire profile is riddled with testimonials)

3. Sell your product. You MUST. Nobody else will do it for you. But the real skill in "selling" is how elegantly you can do it, without it feeling like a pitch. (I always "sell without selling")

Remember my 3 musts: Share. Showcase. Sell.
