## Selection Criteria

Experts were chosen based on:
1. **Proven results** — demonstrable business outcomes tied to LinkedIn organic (revenue, pipeline, company growth)
2. **Specificity** — concrete systems and frameworks, not generic advice
3. **B2B SaaS relevance** — either works at/with SaaS companies or built one using LinkedIn
4. **Content quality** — regular, substantive posts worth analyzing
5. **Diversity of angles** — algorithm, psychology, sales, founder brand, team content, measurement

Deliberately excluded: generic LinkedIn coaches with large followings but no B2B SaaS track record.
